Diet Coke Flavors

Besides the original Diet Coke flavor, there is a caffiene-free version, and these flavors including Ginger lime, Twisted Mango, Zesty Blood Orange, and Feisty Cherry. I also recommend an original Diet Coke with a squeeze of lime or lemon juice!
