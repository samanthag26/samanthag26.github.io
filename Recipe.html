The Diet Coke Recipe + Ingredients

Recipe
Carbonated Water Water infused with pressurized carbon dioxide gas
Caramel Color Produced by heating sugar with ammonium compounds
Aspartame Low-calorie artificial sweetener used widely as a sugar substitute in foods and beverages since the 1980s
Phosphoric Acid A colorless, odorless, non-volatile inorganic mineral acid
Potassium Benzoate A synthetic food preservative used to inhibit the growth of mold, yeast, and bacteria
Natural Flavors Mix of citrus oils, cinnamon, and vanilla
Citric Acid Citric acid is a weak organic acid naturally found in citrus fruits like lemons and limes. Used as a preservative for drinks and foods.
Caffeine A natural central nervous system stimulant found in coffee, tea, cacao, and energy drinks
